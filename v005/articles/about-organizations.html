About Organizations

Organizations are great for businesses and large open-source projects that need multiple owners and administrators, grouped together with teams that determine affinity and repository permissions.

Organizations include:

  • Business plans for private repositories
  • Team-based repository access permissions
  • Unlimited owners, administrators, and collaborators using teams
  • Owners team access to organization members’ two-factor authentication (2FA) status
